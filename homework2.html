Considerations

Interesting things happen with the new changes. With absolute frequencies the variance seems to grow linearly with time, ending in the neighborhood of the number of systems at the end when p is 0.5. Sperimentally, I think the expected variance is equal to the number of systems in this case. With the relative frequencies, the variance seems to stop growing linearly but “stabilizes” after a number of steps and changes slighty after that.
